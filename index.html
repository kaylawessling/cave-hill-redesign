About

Welcome to Cave Hill Cemetery, a Louisville landmark and one of the most prestegious historic cemeteries in the United States. We invite you to look around. Explore our history. Search for loved ones. Learn about our preservation initiatives. And, if you're so inclined, inquire if Cave Hill is the right cemetery for your after-life care.
